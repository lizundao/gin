**gin.wiki** is an **unofficial** documentation mirror. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or maintained by the Gin project or its authors.

## What this site is

The content here is synced from the official Gin documentation at [gin-gonic/website](https://github.com/gin-gonic/website). We do not write or own that material. All documentation copyright remains with the Gin project and its contributors.

The only reason this site exists is practical: **gin.wiki** is a short, easy domain to remember and type when you want to look something up. That is all.

## Official sources

- Official documentation: [gin-gonic.com](https://gin-gonic.com)
- Source repository: [github.com/gin-gonic/website](https://github.com/gin-gonic/website)
- Gin framework: [github.com/gin-gonic/gin](https://github.com/gin-gonic/gin)

If you believe any content on this site infringes your rights, please contact us at [contact@gin.wiki](mailto:contact@gin.wiki). We will review your request promptly and remove the relevant content if appropriate.
